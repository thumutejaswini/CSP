B.E

The full form of B.E. is Bachelor of Engineering and it is an undergraduate program with a duration of four years. A B.E course promotes theoretical understanding as well as the engineering component of science

B.E: Course Highlights

B.E Fee Structure

B.E. program fees vary depending on the college or university that you attend. The average fee for the B.E. program ranges between Rs. 4,00,000 to Rs. 8,00,000.

Skills Required for B.E. Degree

Candidates who are pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering need to have a certain skill set. These skills help the candidate to get a good position in the top organisations.

B.E. Application Process

Generally, the admission process is done on the basis of entrance examinations conducted all over the country. The admission process may vary from one college to another college. The admission process can be divided into two types: entrance based and merit-based.

Top Entrance Examinations for B.E. degree

  • JEE Main
  • JEE Advanced
  • VITEEE(Vellore Institute of Technology)
  • BITSAT(Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani)

B.E. Syllabus

Bachelor of engineering subjects and syllabus is divided into four years with eight semesters. The Bachelor of Engineering subjects and syllabus are also divided into eight semesters including lab sessions. Each semester has different subjects for every branch except the first-year syllabus. The students will mainly concentrate on subjects related to physics, chemistry, and other programming languages.

What after B.E.?

Candidates after completing their B.E. can go for higher studies to improvise their technical skills such as M.Tech. Candidates can get a good position after completing M.Tech from a top and reputed university.
