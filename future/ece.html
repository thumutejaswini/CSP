ECE

The full form of ECE is Electronics and Communication Engineering. ECE is an engineering branch that includes the production, design, research and testing of electronic devices and technology issues related to telecommunications systems, electronics, computers and related industrial sectors. ECE is introduced at the UG and PG standard by several colleges and universities in India, referring to B.Tech and M.Tech degrees, respectively.

Eligibility criteria for ECE Engineering course

To get admission in ECE engineering course, the applicants must clear the 12th class with science subject exams such as Chemistry, Mathematics and Physics.

Career Pathways after Electronics and Communication Engineering

  • Service Engineer.
  • Network Planning Engineer.
  • Communication Engineer.
  • Telecom Engineer.
  • Desktop Support Engineer.
  • Software Analyst.
