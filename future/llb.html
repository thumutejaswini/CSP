Bachelor of Laws (LLB)

The Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Law, also known as the BA LLB, is a five-year professional undergraduate degree that can be earned after passing the 10+2 exam or passing a recognized board.

LLB Highlights

LLB Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed a recognized board's 10+2 or equivalent exam

Skills Requirements for a LLB

  • Research Skills
  • Confidence
  • Power of Persuasion
  • Time Management Skills
  • Good Judgment Skills
  • Objectivity
