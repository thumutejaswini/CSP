<h2 style="text-align:center">Bachelor of Veterinary Science </h2>
<p>Bachelor in Veterinary Science is an undergraduate degree programme which is also known as B.V.Sc, a degree for studies in veterinary medicine. Bachelor of Veterinary Science, BVSC course duration is 5.5 years with a compulsory internship programme. Bachelor of Veterinary Science (B.V.Sc) deals with the study of medical diagnostics, and the treatment of diseases of all animals.</p>
