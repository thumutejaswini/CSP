A Career in X-Ray Technology

The healthcare industry is constantly expanding and progressing into the future. The demand for X-ray technology has increased as the community seeks better ways to detect health problems. Your career as an X-ray technician can begin with a diploma in X-ray technology or a graduate degree in Radiology. Earning an X-ray technology diploma serves as a foundation for other certifications, such as advanced radiography certifications and specialised BSc and health administration degrees. Whether you're looking for a certificate programme or an associate, bachelor's, or master's degree in radiologic technology, being an X-ray technician can lead to a variety of lucrative job opportunities.
